Project Timeline

Now – Late 2025

Pre-Construction: Final design detailing, contractor selection, site surveys, ground investigations.

Q1 2026

Site Set-Up & Groundworks: Site clearance, drainage, foundation pouring, initial ground levels established.

Q2 2026

Structural Build: Erection of frame, external walls, roof installation, windows and doors fitted.

Q3 2026

First Fix Works: Plumbing and electrics installation, insulation, internal wall structures completed.

Q3 2026

External Works: Roofing finishes, rendering, cladding, external landscape structures begun.

Q4 2026

Second Fix Works: Internal doors hung, kitchens and bathrooms fitted, final plastering and decorating.

Early 2027

Decoration & Landscaping: Final painting, flooring installation, garden works and driveway completed.

Spring 2027

Project Completion & Handover: Snagging, compliance checks, certification, client handover and occupation.
