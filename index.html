About the The Haven Project

A journey to create a forever home — rooted in intention, crafted with care.

Our self-build project at Reigate Road is the realisation of a long-held dream: to create a beautiful, sustainable, and deeply personal home that reflects our values, nurtures our family, and harmonises with the landscape. This journey isn't just about building a house, it’s about crafting a forever home with intention, care, and collaboration. We are embracing the self-build process as an opportunity to work closely with skilled professionals and to bring together traditional craftsmanship with modern innovation.

This website will document our journey, share resources, and serve as a living project hub for everyone involved.

Financial Overview

A responsible, transparent approach to funding our dream, balancing ambition with sustainability.

Creating a forever home is not just an emotional commitment, it's a financial one. We believe that strong foundations aren't only built in brick and timber, but also in careful planning, honest budgeting, and mindful stewardship of resources.

Below is a high-level overview of our projected costs and financial strategy for this self-build project. These figures reflect both the opportunities we are creating, and the responsibilities we are honoring throughout this journey.

(Note: All figures are preliminary estimates and may evolve as design, approvals, and construction proceed.)

The Haven Project Timeline

Now – Late 2025

Pre-Construction: Final design detailing, contractor selection, site surveys, ground investigations.

Q1 2026

Site Set-Up & Groundworks: Site clearance, drainage, foundation pouring, initial ground levels established.

Q2 2026

Structural Build: Erection of frame, external walls, roof installation, windows and doors fitted.

Q3 2026

First Fix Works: Plumbing and electrics installation, insulation, internal wall structures completed.

Q3 2026

External Works: Roofing finishes, rendering, cladding, external landscape structures begun.

Q4 2026

Second Fix Works: Internal doors hung, kitchens and bathrooms fitted, final plastering and decorating.

Early 2027

Decoration & Landscaping: Final painting, flooring installation, garden works and driveway completed.

Spring 2027

Project Completion & Handover: Snagging, compliance checks, certification, client handover and occupation.
