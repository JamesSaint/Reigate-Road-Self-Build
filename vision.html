Our Vision

Reigate Road is more than a self-build; it is a future home designed with a deep respect for nature, sustainability, and modern living. The home will be positioned to maximise sunlight, river views, and green space.

The design embraces open-plan living, energy efficiency, and seamless flow between indoor and outdoor spaces, all set within over an acre of landscaped grounds.

Local Environment

Located in Horley, Surrey — a thriving town with excellent schools, amenities, and fast transport links to London, Gatwick, and the South Coast. Ideal for families, commuters, and those seeking a blend of town and country life.

Nearby you’ll find riverside walks, woodland trails, and vibrant local community facilities.
